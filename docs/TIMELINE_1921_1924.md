# Timeline 1921–1924

## Implemented vertical slice

- **March 1921:** Kronstadt suppression; Tenth Party Congress; NEP turn; unity resolution; Workers' Opposition condemnation and strategic response.
- **April 1921:** tax-in-kind/NEP debate; Volga famine warnings; Petrograd labor crisis; covert reorganization.
- **May 1921:** Cheka investigation; union congress arena; Kollontai publication dispute; Tambov crisis.
- **June 1921:** Donbas factory committees; leadership exhaustion/absence rumors; composite Central Committee labor-policy vote.
- **July 1921:** private trade/NEPmen; famine escalation and foreign relief; Myasnikov dispute; security raid.
- **August 1921:** internal conference and six-month assessment; chapter outcome.

## Expansion anchors (not yet implemented)

- **Late 1921:** relief expansion, NEP administration, party discipline, regional consolidation.
- **1922:** GPU replaces Cheka; Stalin becomes General Secretary in April; Letter of the Twenty-Two; Lenin's illness; USSR formed in December.
- **1923:** economic recovery and scissors crisis; Left Opposition formation; nationality and bureaucracy disputes.
- **Early 1924:** Lenin's death in January; succession struggle becomes explicit; campaign endpoint in April.

Later anchors are architectural guidance only and require new sourced content before play.
