# Regional Geography

The map is a coherent geographic gameplay projection of the former Russian Empire and adjacent strategic context in 1921. It is not a cadastral or archival boundary map.

## Selectable regions

The 28 stable Phase Two IDs remain intact. Each polygon is a strategic composite tied to the same simulation record, operations, institutions, and saves as before. The new shapes follow a common longitude/latitude projection and share edges where practical. Adjacency metadata supports selected-region focus and immediate-neighbor comparison.

Regional data continues to track population in thousands, economic role, composition, administration, faction influence, movements, soviet/union/factory organization, Cheka and Red Army presence, output, food/famine, infrastructure, unrest, worker support, and intelligence confidence.

## Context and infrastructure

- Public-domain Natural Earth 1:110m country outlines provide coastlines and surrounding land context.
- Twenty-one city records use period-appropriate primary labels where relevant, including Petrograd, Tiflis, and Novo-Nikolayevsk. Modern names appear only in notes/tooltips.
- Six major rivers and five strategic rail systems are contextual lines rather than selectable simulation regions.
- Baltic, Black, Caspian, and Arctic labels orient the player at national zoom.

## Known simplifications

- Borders are strategic composites, not exact guberniya, oblast, ASSR, SSR, military-front, or Far Eastern Republic lines for a single archival date.
- Kharkov remains grouped with the Donbas gameplay theater.
- The Baltic Frontier is an operational border zone.
- Siberia, Kazakhstan, and Turkestan are heavily aggregated.
- The Caucasus is enlarged enough for selection and comparison.
- Georgia's March description reflects the newly Sovietized republic after the invasion.
- River and railway paths are generalized for the 1000×560 map and do not claim surveyed alignment.

All geographic simplifications are presentation changes only; the simulation's March–August 1921 balance and IDs are unchanged.
